## Personal Income Tax

Three schedular categories: employment income, business income, and investment income, each taxed differently.
Tax year = calendar year. Filing deadline: 30 April of the following year for those earning ≥ ALL 2,000,000.
Employers withhold tax at source monthly (20th of following month). (EY pp. 18, 20–21)

### Employment Income Brackets

Employment income is subject to a two-rate progressive tax applied to **taxable income** (after deductions). (EY p. 18)

| Taxable annual income (ALL) | Tax rate | Cumulative tax at bracket top |
|---|---|---|
| First ALL 2,040,000 | **13%** | ALL 265,200 |
| Above ALL 2,040,000 | **23%** | — |

**Standard deductions for employment income (EY p. 18):**

| Annual gross salary | Deductible amount | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ≤ ALL 600,000 | ALL 600,000 | Full gross deducted → effective 0% |
| ALL 600,001 – 720,000 | ALL 420,000 | Partial deduction |
| > ALL 720,000 | ALL 360,000 | Flat deduction for all higher earners |
| Per child under 18 | ALL 48,000 | Additional child deduction |
| Tuition (if annual income ≤ ALL 1.2M) | Up to ALL 100,000 | For children's education |

**Effective bracket structure in terms of gross salary:**
- Gross ≤ ALL 600,000/year (~€5,800): effective **0%** (deduction covers full income)
- Gross ALL 600,001 – ~2,400,000/year: **13%** on taxable portion (taxable = gross − 360,000)
- Gross above ~2,400,000/year (~€23,100): **23%** on taxable portion above ALL 2,040,000

**Note on brackets:** EY (October 2025) and PWC confirm the above brackets. The tatime.gov.al monthly withholding table shows: ALL 0–30,000/month (0%), ALL 30,001–150,000/month (13%), above ALL 150,000/month (23%). Annualized: 0–360k/1.8M/above. Discrepancy likely reflects monthly-vs-annual reconciliation and the deduction system; see Known Gaps.

### Business Income (Self-Employed / Sole Traders — Standard Regime)

| Annual profit (ALL) | Tax rate |
|---|---|
| First ALL 14,000,000 | **15%** (standard; **0% until 31 December 2029** — see below) |
| Above ALL 14,000,000 | **23%** |

**Person Fizik — 0% regime (in force 2026):**
Self-employed individuals and sole traders with annual **gross turnover ≤ ALL 14,000,000** are subject to **0% income tax** until 31 December 2029. (EY p. 18; confirmed by taxsummaries.pwc.com)
- Tax base: net profit (gross income minus documented business expenses)
- 0% applies regardless of net profit (as long as gross turnover ≤ ALL 14M)
- After 2029: reverts to 15%/23% progressive scale

**Alternative expense deduction (for gross ≤ ALL 10M, no bookkeeping required):**

| Activity | Deductible expense % of revenue |
|---|---|
| Production activities | 60% |
| Wholesale trade | 90% |
| Retail trade / individual transport | 70% |
| Bars, restaurants, discos | 60% |
| Services and crafts | 50% |
| **Professional self-employed services** | **30%** |

(EY p. 15) — Under the 0% regime these deductions are academic (tax = 0 anyway); relevant only if status is lost after 2029.

### Investment Income

| Income type | Rate |
|---|---|
| Dividends | **8%** (withholding; final) |
| All other investment income (interest, royalties, rentals, capital gains from securities/real estate) | **15%** |
| Inheritance / donations (outside 1st/2nd degree relatives or siblings) | **15%** |
| Gambling winnings | **15%** |

No deductions apply to investment income. (EY pp. 17–18)

## Social Security

**Employment contributions (EY p. 20):**

| Contribution type | Employee | Employer | Total |
|---|---|---|---|
| Social security (pension/disability) | **9.5%** | **15.0%** | 24.5% |
| Health insurance | **1.7%** | **1.7%** | 3.4% |
| **Total** | **11.2%** | **16.7%** | **27.9%** |

- Minimum monthly remuneration base: ALL 40,000/month (minimum salary)
- **Maximum monthly base for employees:** ALL 176,416/month (= ALL 2,116,992/year)
- Above cap: no SS contributions on the excess
- SS is calculated on gross salary within the cap range

**Self-employed social security (EY p. 20):**

| Contribution | Rate | Base | Monthly | Annual |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Social security | 23% | Minimum salary (ALL 40,000) | ALL 9,200 | ALL 110,400 |
| Health insurance | 6.8% | Minimum salary (ALL 40,000) | ALL 2,720 | ALL 32,640 |
| **Total** | **29.8%** | | **ALL 11,920** | **ALL 143,040** |

- Fixed amount regardless of actual income (based only on minimum salary of ALL 40,000/month)
- Self-employed pay on the minimum salary base only — no income-proportional scaling

## Self-Employment Regimes

### Option A — Person Fizik with 0% Income Tax (until 31 December 2029)

- **Who qualifies:** Any individual registered as a self-employed person / sole trader at the National Business Centre (Qendra Kombëtare e Biznesit) with annual **gross turnover ≤ ALL 14,000,000** (~€134,600 at ALL 104/EUR). Applies to services, trade, production, and professional activities including IT.
- **Tax base:** Net profit (gross revenue minus documented business expenses), but **tax rate = 0%** on all net profit while gross turnover ≤ ALL 14,000,000.
- **Rate / mechanism:**
  - **0% income tax** on all business income — confirmed in force through 31 December 2029
  - Standard rate reverts to 15% (first ALL 14M of profit) and 23% (above) after 2029
  - Alternative flat expense deduction (30% for professional services, 50% for services/crafts) available if gross < ALL 10M and no bookkeeping preferred — but irrelevant while at 0%
- **Social security:** Fixed minimum: ALL 9,200 SS + ALL 2,720 health = **ALL 11,920/month = ALL 143,040/year** regardless of income
- **VAT obligations:**
  - VAT registration threshold: ALL 10,000,000/year turnover
  - Standard VAT rate: 20%
  - Below ALL 10M: no VAT registration required (but optional registration permitted)
- **Key limits:**
  - Gross turnover must not exceed ALL 14,000,000/year for 0% regime
  - If turnover exceeds ALL 14,000,000: excess income taxable at 15%/23% (the threshold is a hard cutover, not a graduated one)
  - Regime expires 31 December 2029; no extension announced as of October 2025

(EY pp. 18–19; taxsummaries.pwc.com Albania)

**Note:** Below ALL 10,000,000 gross turnover, the taxpayer may elect NOT to keep detailed books and instead use the fixed expense percentage (30% for professional services). Combined with 0% income tax, this makes compliance very light for IT/professional freelancers until 2029.

## Special Regimes for Newcomers / Expats

### EU Citizens — Single Permit (Law No. 43/2025)

- **Effective:** 25 July 2025 (entered into force)
- **Key provision:** EU citizens who are employed or self-employed in Albania are **exempt** from the single-permit requirement for the right to work (no prior work permit needed)
- EU citizens staying >3 months must register online with the border/migration authority within 3 months of arrival → receive Registration Certificate
- Implementation not yet started as of EY data date (EY pp. 22–23)

### Open Balkans Initiative

- Albania, North Macedonia, and Serbia citizens have simplified mutual work/residency access via the Open Balkans portal (e-Albania). Two-year access document issued. (EY p. 23)

### No Dedicated Newcomer Tax Regime

Albania does not have a remittance-basis, territorial-tax, or new-resident PIT exemption. Foreign tax residents arriving in Albania become subject to standard Albanian PIT on worldwide income from the date they establish residency.

## 2026 Changes

- **Person Fizik 0% regime continues (confirmed):** In force until 31 December 2029 for gross turnover ≤ ALL 14,000,000. No changes announced for 2026. (EY p. 18; PWC)
- **EU citizens work-permit exemption:** Law No. 43/2025 (effective 25 July 2025) exempts EU citizens from single-permit requirement; implementation is ongoing (EY p. 22)
- **No PIT rate changes for 2026** announced as of October 2025.
- **No changes to SS rates** announced.
- **Minimum salary:** EY cites minimum monthly salary ALL 40,000; verify for any 2026 upward adjustment as this would change self-employed SS minimum.

## Effective Rate Examples

> All figures are estimates. Single person, no dependants, no children, no tuition deduction.
> **1 EUR = ALL 104** (approximate; consistent with user reference ALL 14M ≈ €135k; verify at bankofalbania.org).
> "Employment" = gross salary (employee-side only; employer's 16.7% SS not shown).
> "Person Fizik" = gross revenue, 0% income tax, fixed SS contributions, no VAT (below ALL 10M threshold at all income points).
> Employment deduction: ALL 360,000 (salary > ALL 720,000).
> SS cap for employment: ALL 176,416/month = ALL 2,116,992/year.

### Working: ALL 3,120,000 (≈ €30,000) Employment

| Step | Calculation | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Deduction | ALL 360,000 (salary > ALL 720,000) | ALL 360,000 |
| Taxable income | 3,120,000 − 360,000 | ALL 2,760,000 |
| PIT on first ALL 2,040,000 (13%) | 13% × 2,040,000 | ALL 265,200 |
| PIT on excess (23%) | 23% × (2,760,000 − 2,040,000) = 23% × 720,000 | ALL 165,600 |
| Total PIT | 265,200 + 165,600 | ALL 430,800 |
| Monthly salary | 3,120,000 / 12 = ALL 260,000 > cap ALL 176,416 | capped |
| Employee SS (11.2% on cap) | 11.2% × 176,416 × 12 | ALL 237,102 |
| **Total burden** | 430,800 + 237,102 | **ALL 667,902** |
| **Effective rate** | 667,902 / 3,120,000 | **21.4%** |

### Working: ALL 3,120,000 (≈ €30,000) Person Fizik 0%

| Step | Calculation | Result |
|---|---|---|
| Income tax (0% regime) | 0% × any amount | ALL 0 |
| SS — social security (fixed) | 23% × 40,000 × 12 | ALL 110,400 |
| SS — health insurance (fixed) | 6.8% × 40,000 × 12 | ALL 32,640 |
| **Total burden** | 0 + 110,400 + 32,640 | **ALL 143,040** |
| **Effective rate** | 143,040 / 3,120,000 | **4.6%** |

### Summary table

| Gross income (ALL) | EUR equiv | Scenario | PIT | SS (employee) | Total | **Effective rate** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ALL 3,120,000 | €30,000 | Employment (13%/23% + deduction) | ALL 430,800 | ALL 237,102 | ALL 667,902 | **21.4%** |
| ALL 3,120,000 | €30,000 | Person Fizik 0% + fixed SS | ALL 0 | ALL 143,040 | ALL 143,040 | **4.6%** |
| ALL 6,240,000 | €60,000 | Employment (13%/23% + deduction) | ALL 1,148,400 | ALL 237,102 | ALL 1,385,502 | **22.2%** |
| ALL 6,240,000 | €60,000 | Person Fizik 0% + fixed SS | ALL 0 | ALL 143,040 | ALL 143,040 | **2.3%** |
| ALL 10,400,000 | €100,000 | Employment (13%/23% + deduction) | ALL 2,105,200 | ALL 237,102 | ALL 2,342,302 | **22.5%** |
| ALL 10,400,000 | €100,000 | Person Fizik 0% + fixed SS | ALL 0 | ALL 143,040 | ALL 143,040 | **1.4%** |

All Person Fizik income points are within the ALL 14,000,000 gross limit (~€134,600).

**Employment PIT working (€60k and €100k):**
- €60k = ALL 6,240,000: taxable = 5,880,000 → PIT = 265,200 + 23% × 3,840,000 = 265,200 + 883,200 = 1,148,400
- €100k = ALL 10,400,000: taxable = 10,040,000 → PIT = 265,200 + 23% × 8,000,000 = 265,200 + 1,840,000 = 2,105,200

**Key insight — Person Fizik advantage is extreme:** With 0% income tax until end of 2029, the only cost is fixed SS of ALL 143,040/year (~€1,375). At €100k income, effective rate is just 1.4%. At €30k it's 4.6% — all from mandatory social security, not income tax. Employment is 21–23%. This is one of the most favorable self-employment regimes in Europe, and is explicitly time-limited (expires 31.12.2029).

**Post-2029 rate (for planning):** After the 0% exemption expires, the standard 15% rate applies on net profit up to ALL 14M. At €30k with 30% expense deduction (professional services), taxable ≈ 70% × 3,120,000 = 2,184,000, tax = 15% × 2,184,000 = ALL 327,600, + SS 143,040 = ALL 470,640 → effective 15.1%. Still favorable versus employment.

**Assumptions:**
- EUR/ALL = 104 (approximation; Bank of Albania official rate; verify at bankofalbania.org)
- Employment: ALL 360,000 deduction applied; no child deductions
- SS cap for employment: ALL 176,416/month (EY p. 20); all income levels exceed cap
- Person Fizik: minimum salary ALL 40,000/month; fixed SS regardless of income
- No VAT: all PF income points below ALL 10,000,000 VAT threshold (€30k and €60k); at €100k = ALL 10,400,000 slightly above threshold — VAT registration may be needed (compliance cost, not tax cost for B2B exports)
- Employer-side SS (16.7%) not shown in employment scenario

## Known Gaps

- [ ] **Employment bracket discrepancy:** EY (Oct 2025) and PWC both show taxable-income brackets of ALL 2,040,000 / 23%, while the user's initial brief stated "0% to ALL 600k, 13% to 3M, 23% above" and tatime.gov.al shows monthly brackets (30k/150k). These produce different effective rates — needs reconciliation against the Albanian Tax Code. All calculations above use EY/PWC brackets.
- [ ] **Minimum salary 2026:** EY cites ALL 40,000/month; if Albania raised the minimum salary for 2026 (it has been periodically adjusted), the self-employed SS fixed contribution would increase. Verify at instat.gov.al or tatime.gov.al.
- [ ] **VAT at €100k income level:** ALL 10,400,000 exceeds the VAT registration threshold of ALL 10,000,000. If services are exported to foreign B2B clients, 0% VAT (zero-rated export) may apply — but registration is still required above threshold. Clarify place-of-supply rules for IT services with an Albanian accountant.
- [ ] **Person Fizik qualified activities:** EY does not list excluded activities for the 0% regime; assume IT/software permitted as there is no stated exclusion. Verify from the full text of the enabling law via tatime.gov.al.
- [ ] **Post-2029 transition planning:** The 0% regime expires 31.12.2029. No extension announced. After expiry, the 15%/23% business income scale applies on net profit. This significantly changes the calculus for those planning multi-year presence in Albania.
- [ ] **EU single permit implementation:** Law No. 43/2025 entered force 25 July 2025 but EY notes "implementation has not yet started." Practical effect on EU citizen freelancers in Albania unclear until full implementation.
- [ ] **Alternative deduction scheme for professional services (30%):** Under 0% regime this is irrelevant; after 2029 expiry, the 30% expense deduction (for professional/consulting services under ALL 10M gross) would reduce taxable base from gross to 70% × gross before applying 15% rate. Model this separately for post-2029 planning.
