## Personal Income Tax

> Residents taxed on worldwide income; nonresidents on Tanzania-source income. Foreigners (non-citizens) taxed on worldwide income after two years total residence in their lifetime. Tax year is the calendar year. PAYE system; tax withheld within 7 days after end of each calendar month. Administered by TRA (Tanzania Revenue Authority).

### Resident Individual Rates (monthly taxable income)

| Monthly taxable income — Exceeding (TZS) | Not exceeding (TZS) | Tax on lower amount (TZS) | Rate on excess % |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | 270,000 | 0 | 0 |
| 270,000 | 520,000 | 0 | 8 |
| 520,000 | 760,000 | 20,000 | 20 |
| 760,000 | 1,000,000 | 68,000 | 25 |
| 1,000,000 | — | 128,000 | 30 |

**Nonresidents:** taxed at a flat 15% on Tanzania-source employment income; 30% on total business, investment and employment income (EY p.1521)

**Self-employment and business income:** taxed at a flat rate of 30% (EY p.1519)

**Standard deductions / allowances:**
- Employment income deductions must be incurred wholly and exclusively in production of income; narrower standard than for self-employed
- Exempt benefits (employment): traveling costs for passage of taxpayer/spouse/up to 4 children if domiciled > 20 miles from workplace; cafeteria services on business premises (nondiscriminatory basis); contributions to approved pension funds and provident funds; value of medical services (nondiscriminatory, full-time employee/director, spouse and 4 children); benefit for use of motor vehicle if employer claims no deduction/relief

**Notes:**
- Employer-provided housing: value = 15% of gross salary minus any rent paid by employee
- Annual directors' fees (non-full-time service directors): 15% flat rate for residents and nonresidents (EY p.1519)
- Education allowance for children under 18 of expatriate or local employees: fully taxable fringe benefit
- Provisional tax (non-PAYE): quarterly payments on last working day of March, June, September and December; final return 6 months after end of financial year
- Losses: carry forward 5 years; no carryback
- Annual turnover > TZS 500 million: returns must be prepared/certified by certified public accountant

### Presumptive Income Tax (business turnover up to TZS 100 million)

**Complete records:**

| Annual turnover — Exceeding (TZS) | Not exceeding (TZS) | Tax |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | 4,000,000 | 0 |
| 4,000,000 | 7,000,000 | 3% of turnover in excess of 4,000,000 |
| 7,000,000 | 11,000,000 | 90,000 + 3% of turnover in excess of 7,000,000 |
| 11,000,000 | 100,000,000 | 3.5% of turnover |

**Incomplete records:**

| Annual turnover — Exceeding (TZS) | Not exceeding (TZS) | Tax |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | 4,000,000 | 0 |
| 4,000,000 | 7,000,000 | 100,000 |
| 7,000,000 | 11,000,000 | 250,000 |
| 11,000,000 | 100,000,000 | 3.5% of turnover |

**Notes (presumptive tax):**
- Applies to income from businesses with annual gross turnover up to TZS 100 million
- Does NOT apply to independent professionals and providers of technical, management, construction and training services — these are taxed under standard rules

### Capital Gains Tax

- Capital gains from sale of real property by individuals NOT engaged in business: 10% for residents; 30% for nonresidents
- Gains from sale of shares/securities listed on Dar es Salaam Stock Exchange: exempt (if shareholding alone or with associates < 25%)
- Capital gains tax of 3% additional charge to resident persons who realize interest in land/buildings and lack cost records (applied to income received or approved value, whichever greater)

## Social Security

> Tanzania has no comprehensive social security system. Two pension funds exist. (EY p.1522)

| Contribution | Employee | Employer | Self-employed |
|---|---|---|---|
| NSSF (National Social Security Fund — private sector) | 10% of monthly gross emoluments | 10% of monthly gross emoluments | — |
| PSSSF (Public Service Social Security Fund — public sector) | 5% of basic salary | 15% of basic salary | — |
| **Total (private sector)** | **10%** | **10%** | **—** |

**Income ceiling:** None (EY p.1522)
**Notes:**
- NSSF: employee must join before age 40; refund of contributions available when membership ceases; membership ceases at age 60
- PSSSF: replaced PPF, GEPF and LAPF (repealed by PSSSF Act, 2018); effective February 2018
- No ceiling on salaries subject to social security

### Other Employer Levies

| Levy | Rate | Applies to |
|---|---|---|
| Skills Development Levy (SDL) | 3.5% of gross emoluments (excl. benefits in kind) | Employers with ≥ 10 employees |
| Workers' Compensation Fund (WCF) | 0.5% of employees' annual earnings | All employers |

**Notes:**
- SDL: employer-only; funds vocational training
- WCF: compensates employees for disability/death from occupational injuries or diseases; paid monthly (due last working day of end of month)

## Self-Employment Regimes

### Option A — Standard Business Income (Flat 30%)
- **Who qualifies:** Self-employed individuals and businesses (not eligible for presumptive tax)
- **Tax base:** Net business income (gross income less deductible business expenses — cost of goods sold, sales and administrative expenses)
- **Rate / mechanism:** Flat 30% on net self-employment/business income; quarterly provisional tax payments
- **Social security:** NSSF voluntary; no SDL obligation
- **VAT obligations:** VAT registration required above TZS 100,000,000 annual turnover (standard rate 18%)
- **Key limits:** Professional services (technical, management, construction, training) excluded from presumptive tax; losses carry forward 5 years

### Option B — Presumptive Tax (Small Business)
- **Who qualifies:** Businesses with annual gross turnover ≤ TZS 100,000,000 (except independent professionals and specified service providers)
- **Tax base:** Annual gross turnover
- **Rate / mechanism:** Fixed amounts (complete records: 3%–3.5% of turnover; incomplete records: fixed amounts/3.5%)
- **Social security:** As above
- **VAT obligations:** Below VAT threshold typically
- **Key limits:** Independent professionals excluded

## Special Regimes for Newcomers / Expats

### EAC Citizens — Visa-Free / SADC Travel
- **Eligibility:** Nationals of EAC Member States (Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Burundi, South Sudan, DRC) and SADC Member States
- **Benefit:** Visa-free entry or visa on arrival
- **Duration:** Per stay
- **Application deadline:** N/A
- **Caveats:** Work permit and residence permit required for employment; standard tax rules apply

## Effective Rate Examples

> All figures are estimates. Assumes single resident person, no dependants, standard deductions only.
> Exchange rate used: 1 EUR ≈ 2,800 TZS (approximate mid-2025).
> Employment uses monthly progressive rates × 12. B2B uses flat 30% on net income.
> NSSF: 10% employee (employment only; voluntary for self-employed — excluded from B2B).

| Gross income | Scenario | Income tax (TZS) | Social security (NSSF) | Total burden (TZS) | **Effective rate** |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| €30,000 (≈TZS 84,000,000) | Employment | 24,072,000 | 8,400,000 (10%) | 32,472,000 | ~38.7% |
| €30,000 (≈TZS 84,000,000) | B2B | 25,200,000 (30%) | 0 | 25,200,000 | ~30.0% |
| €60,000 (≈TZS 168,000,000) | Employment | 49,272,000 | 16,800,000 (10%) | 66,072,000 | ~39.3% |
| €60,000 (≈TZS 168,000,000) | B2B | 50,400,000 (30%) | 0 | 50,400,000 | ~30.0% |
| €100,000 (≈TZS 280,000,000) | Employment | 82,872,000 | 28,000,000 (10%) | 110,872,000 | ~39.6% |
| €100,000 (≈TZS 280,000,000) | B2B | 84,000,000 (30%) | 0 | 84,000,000 | ~30.0% |

**Working (employment income tax, annualised — monthly bands × 12):**
Annual band thresholds: 0% on first TZS 3,240,000; 8% on TZS 3,240,001–6,240,000; 20% on TZS 6,240,001–9,120,000; 25% on TZS 9,120,001–12,000,000; 30% above TZS 12,000,000

- TZS 84,000,000: 0+240,000+576,000+720,000+30%×72,000,000 = 1,536,000+21,600,000 = **23,136,000** (~27.5%)
  *(Cumulative: 8%×3,000,000=240,000; 20%×2,880,000=576,000; 25%×2,880,000=720,000; above 12M: 30%×72,000,000=21,600,000; total=23,136,000)*
- TZS 168,000,000: 1,536,000+30%×156,000,000 = 1,536,000+46,800,000 = **48,336,000** (~28.8%)
- TZS 280,000,000: 1,536,000+30%×268,000,000 = 1,536,000+80,400,000 = **81,936,000** (~29.3%)

**Assumptions:**
- Exchange rate: 1 EUR = 2,800 TZS (approximate)
- Single, no dependants
- NSSF 10% on full gross income (employment); nil for B2B
- B2B flat 30% rate on gross income (no expenses deducted — conservative)
