## Personal Income Tax

Residents taxed on worldwide income; non-residents on Azerbaijani-source income only. Resident = present in Azerbaijan ≥ 183 days in a calendar year. Tax year = calendar year. (EY p.104)

Azerbaijan has a **dual-sector PIT structure** — different rates apply to oil/gas and public sector versus non-oil/gas and non-public sector employees.

**Oil and gas / public sector employees:**

| Monthly taxable income (AZN) | Tax rate | Tax due (AZN) | Cumulative tax due (AZN) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to 2,500 | **14%** | 350 | 350 |
| Above 2,500 | **25%** | — | — |

Individuals with monthly gross salary < AZN 2,500 are exempt from tax on the first AZN 200 of monthly salary. (EY p.105)

**Non-oil/gas and non-public sector employees** (effective from 1 January 2019, for a period of seven years):

| Monthly taxable income (AZN) | Tax rate | Tax due (AZN) | Cumulative tax due (AZN) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Up to 8,000 | **0%** | 0 | 0 |
| Above 8,000 | **14%** | — | — |

(EY p.105)

**Non-entrepreneurial activities** (dividends, interest, royalties, capital gains from non-business assets):
- **14%** flat personal income tax rate (EY p.105)

**Entrepreneurial activities** (individuals conducting business without creating a legal entity):
- **20%** flat rate on taxable income (EY p.105)

**Capital gains:**
- Capital gains from the sale of most movable tangible property not used for business purposes: **exempt** from tax in Azerbaijan (EY p.104)

**Standard deductions / allowances:**
- Monthly personal allowance: individuals with 3+ dependants (including students under 23) receive a reduction of AZN 50/month on taxable income (EY p.105)
- Employment expenses: the cost of employer-provided/reimbursed accommodation, food, and business trip expenses are exempt (EY p.104)
- Business deductions (entrepreneurial): materials, amortisation, lease payments, wages, state social security, therapeutic nourishment, milk, interest, capital production asset repairs (within established limits) (EY p.105)
- Gifts/assistance for education up to AZN 2,500: exempt
- Medical treatment assistance in Azerbaijan up to AZN 10,000; abroad up to AZN 50,000: exempt
- Inheritances up to AZN 20,000: exempt; from family members: exempt without limit (EY p.105)

**Notes:**
- Loss carry-forward: up to 3 years for entrepreneurial activities (EY p.105)
- No estate or gift tax in Azerbaijan (EY p.105)
- PAYE withheld by employers; employees with employment income only need not file returns
- Self-employed: quarterly advance payments (1/4 of prior year tax or effective rate × quarterly revenue) by 15 April, 15 July, 15 October, 15 January; annual return by 31 March of following year (EY p.107)

## Social Security

**Oil and gas / public sector:**

| Contribution | Employee | Employer |
|---|---|---|
| Social insurance | **3%** of gross salary | **22%** of gross salary |

(EY p.106)

**Non-oil/gas and non-public sector** (effective from 1 January 2019, for seven years):

For individuals with monthly gross taxable income above AZN 200:
- Employee: **10%** on the amount exceeding AZN 200 plus AZN 6 (i.e., social security withheld from employee = 10% × (monthly salary − AZN 200) + AZN 6 for the amount > AZN 200)
- Employer: **15%** on the amount exceeding AZN 200 plus AZN 44

(EY p.106)

**Unemployment insurance** (additional, on top of social insurance):
- Employee: **0.5%** of gross salary
- Employer: **0.5%** of gross salary
- Total: 1% of gross salary paid to Unemployment Insurance Fund
- Applies to individuals holding local employment agreements (EY p.106)

**Medical insurance** (effective from 1 January 2021):
- Employee: **2%** on salary up to AZN 8,000/month; **0.5%** on the part above AZN 8,000/month
- Must be withheld from employee salaries (EY p.106)

**Summary — non-oil/gas sector employee (monthly salary AZN 2,000 example):**

| Contribution | Employee | Employer |
|---|---|---|
| Social insurance (non-oil) | 10% × (salary − AZN 200) + AZN 6 | 15% × (salary − AZN 200) + AZN 44 |
| Unemployment insurance | 0.5% | 0.5% |
| Medical insurance | 2% (up to AZN 8,000) | — |

**Income ceiling:** AZN 8,000/month for medical insurance (2% below, 0.5% above); no explicit cap stated for social insurance in non-oil sector (EY p.106)

## Self-Employment Regimes

### Option A — Individual Entrepreneur (Entrepreneurial Activity without Legal Entity)

- **Who qualifies:** Any individual carrying out entrepreneurial activities in Azerbaijan without creating a legal entity
- **Tax base:** Gross income less expenses incurred in earning the income (net profit)
- **Rate / mechanism:** **20%** flat rate on taxable profit
- **Social security:** Social insurance and other contributions apply (rate depends on sector)
- **VAT obligations:** VAT registration required if annual turnover exceeds AZN 200,000; VAT rate 18%
- **Key limits:** No revenue ceiling; losses carry-forward up to 3 years
- **Filing:** Quarterly advance payments; annual return by 31 March

(EY p.105, 107)

### Option B — Simplified Tax Regime (for qualifying small entrepreneurs)

- **Who qualifies:** Micro-entrepreneurs meeting criteria under Azerbaijani Tax Code (turnover ≤ AZN 200,000/year, or qualifying activities)
- **Tax base:** Gross revenue
- **Rate / mechanism:** Simplified tax rate (varies by activity and region; typically 2%–4% of revenue)
- **Social security:** Contributions apply
- **VAT obligations:** Not required to register for VAT below AZN 200,000 threshold
- **Key limits:** AZN 200,000/year revenue ceiling

(Note: Simplified tax regime exists under Azerbaijani Tax Code; EY covers general entrepreneurial rate at 20%; simplified regime details from standard Azerbaijani tax legislation)

## Special Regimes for Newcomers / Expats

### Non-Oil/Gas Sector Zero-Rate Band

- **Eligibility:** Employees working in non-oil/gas and non-public sector with monthly taxable income ≤ AZN 8,000/month (~€4,700/month or ~€56,000/year at AZN 1.7/USD and ~1.05 USD/EUR)
- **Benefit:** 0% PIT on all employment income up to AZN 8,000/month (effectively all typical expat employment salaries in non-oil sector)
- **Duration:** 7 years from 1 January 2019 (i.e., through approximately end of 2025/2026 — note: the 7-year period may be expiring; verify current status)
- **Caveats:** Only for non-oil/gas and non-public sector; oil/gas workers pay 14%/25% (EY p.105)

---

## 2026 Changes

- **Non-oil/gas sector 0%/14% rate structure:** Introduced for a period of "seven years" from 1 January 2019. The seven-year period technically expires at end of 2025. Status of this preferential rate for 2026 and beyond is uncertain — critical to verify whether extended or reverted to standard rates. (EY p.105)
- **Multilateral Convention (BEPS):** Azerbaijan signed the Multilateral Convention on 20 November 2023 (approved by Milli Majlis, Law No. 1165-VIQ, 31 May 2024). It entered into force on 1 January 2025, amending existing tax treaties. (EY p.107)
- Exchange rate stated as AZN 1.7 = USD 1 (EY p.104)

## Effective Rate Examples

> All figures are estimates. Single person, no dependants, standard deductions only.
> **1 EUR ≈ AZN 1.84** (AZN 1.7/USD × EUR/USD ~1.08 ≈ AZN 1.84/EUR; approximate).
> Two employment scenarios: non-oil sector (0%/14%) and oil sector (14%/25%).
> B2B scenario: entrepreneurial activity at 20% flat rate.
> **CRITICAL NOTE:** The non-oil 0%/14% structure was introduced for 7 years from Jan 2019 — may expire end of 2025. If not renewed, standard rates (14%/25%) would apply from 2026.
