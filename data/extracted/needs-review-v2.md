# Needs Manual Review — Cross-Verification V2 Discrepancies (PwC vs EY)

Countries flagged here had major discrepancies (>3 percentage points or structural differences) between EY and PwC data during the 2026 round-2 verification against PwC Worldwide Tax Summaries.

| Country | Field | EY value | PwC value | Difference | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| chile-2026 | Top PIT rate | 40% (>310 MTU/month) | 35.50% | 4.5pp | EY shows a top 40% bracket (>310 MTU) which exceeds the standard 35% bracket; PwC page (reviewed Dec 2025) does not show a 40% bracket.
